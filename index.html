Rispondere senza sapere

Discursive Strategies of Large Language Models Under Information Scarcity

Author: Paolo Labruna

Overview

This research work investigates the discursive strategies adopted by Large Language Models when responding to queries under conditions of poor documentation or obscure knowledge domains.

The study focuses on how models handle epistemic uncertainty, including hedging, fabrication, strategic vagueness, hallucinated source attribution and responses produced under information scarcity.

Research Topics
